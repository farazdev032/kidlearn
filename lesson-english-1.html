🔤 What is the Alphabet?

The English alphabet has 26 letters. Every word in English is made from these letters. Learning the alphabet is the very first step to reading and writing!

💡 Did you know? The word "alphabet" comes from the first two Greek letters — Alpha and Beta!

🌟 Special Letters — Vowels!

5 letters are called vowels. They are extra important because almost every word needs one!
