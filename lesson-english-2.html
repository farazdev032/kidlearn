🔊 What is Phonics?

Phonics teaches us the sounds that letters make. Every letter has its own special sound. When we know the sounds, we can read any word!

💡 Remember: The letter name and the letter sound are different! The letter is called "Bee" but its sound is "buh" as in Ball!
