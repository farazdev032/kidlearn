🔡 What are CVC Words?

CVC stands for Consonant — Vowel — Consonant. These are the simplest 3-letter words in English. Every CVC word has a consonant at the start, a vowel in the middle, and a consonant at the end!

💡 Example: The word CAT = C (consonant) + A (vowel) + T (consonant). That is a CVC word!
