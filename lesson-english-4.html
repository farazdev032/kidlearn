📝 What is a Sentence?

A sentence is a group of words that makes complete sense. Every sentence has at least two parts — someone doing something!

💡 Remember: A sentence always starts with a Capital Letter and ends with a Full Stop (.) or Question Mark (?) or Exclamation Mark (!)

🔍 Parts of a Sentence

A simple sentence has 3 parts:

👤
Subject
WHO is doing something
⚡
Verb
WHAT they are doing
🎯
Object
WHAT they are doing it to

📚 Example Sentences — See how they work!

🐱
The cat drinks milk.
👤 The cat = Subject ⚡ drinks = Verb 🎯 milk = Object
👦
Ali reads a book.
👤 Ali = Subject ⚡ reads = Verb 🎯 a book = Object
🐦
The bird sings a song.
👤 The bird = Subject ⚡ sings = Verb 🎯 a song = Object
