📚 What is Reading Comprehension?

Reading Comprehension means reading a piece of writing and understanding what it says. It is not just about reading the words — it is about knowing the meaning!

💡 Top Tips for Good Reading:

1️⃣ Read the whole passage first before answering questions
2️⃣ Read it again slowly and carefully
3️⃣ Look for key words in the questions
4️⃣ Find the answer in the passage — it is always there!

🔍 Key Reading Skills

👀
Skimming
Reading quickly to get the main idea
🔍
Scanning
Looking for specific information
🧠
Understanding
Knowing what the text means
❓
Questioning
Asking who, what, where, when, why

Bina the Busy Bee

Bina is a bee who lives in a big hive in the garden. Every morning, Bina wakes up early and flies to the flowers.


Bina loves to collect honey from the flowers. She visits red roses, yellow sunflowers, and pink tulips. The flowers smell very sweet.


After collecting honey, Bina flies back to her hive. She shares the honey with her friends. All the bees in the hive are very happy.


At night, Bina rests in her hive. She dreams about the beautiful flowers she will visit tomorrow. Bina is a very hardworking and kind bee.
