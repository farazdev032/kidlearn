📝 What is Grammar?

Grammar is the set of rules that tells us how to use words correctly. Today we will learn the three most important types of words — Nouns, Verbs, and Adjectives!

💡 Remember: Every sentence is made up of different types of words. Knowing what each word does helps you write and speak much better!

🔤 The Three Word Types

👤
Noun
A naming word. Names a person, place, animal or thing.
Ali, Lahore, cat, book, school
⚡
Verb
An action word. Tells us what someone or something does.
runs, eats, jumps, reads, sleeps
🎨
Adjective
A describing word. Tells us more about a noun.
big, red, happy, tall, beautiful

📚 See them in a sentence!

The tall boy runs to school.
🎨 tall = Adjective 👤 boy = Noun ⚡ runs = Verb 👤 school = Noun
A beautiful bird sings sweetly.
🎨 beautiful = Adjective 👤 bird = Noun ⚡ sings = Verb
