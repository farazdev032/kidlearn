✍️ What is a Paragraph?

A paragraph is a group of sentences all about one main idea. A good paragraph has three parts — a beginning, a middle, and an end!

💡 Remember: Every paragraph starts with an indent (a small space) or a new line. All sentences in a paragraph must be about the SAME topic!

🏗️ The 3 Parts of a Paragraph

1

Topic Sentence

The FIRST sentence. It tells the reader what the whole paragraph is about. It is the most important sentence!

2

Supporting Sentences

The MIDDLE sentences (2-4 sentences). They give details, examples, and reasons to support the topic sentence.

3

Concluding Sentence

The LAST sentence. It wraps up the paragraph and reminds the reader of the main idea.

📚 Example Paragraph — See the parts!

Dogs make wonderful pets. They are loyal and always happy to see their owners. Dogs can be trained to follow commands and even do tricks. They also help keep their owners active by going on walks every day. For all these reasons, dogs are truly a man's best friend.
