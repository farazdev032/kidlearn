📚 What is a Story?

A story is a piece of writing that tells about events happening to characters. Every good story has a beginning, a middle, and an end!

💡 The Golden Rule of Story Writing: Every story needs a CHARACTER who has a PROBLEM and finds a SOLUTION. That is what makes a story exciting to read!

🏗️ The 3 Parts of a Story

🌅

Beginning — Set the Scene

Introduce your characters and setting. Tell the reader WHO is in the story, WHERE it happens, and WHEN. Make it interesting so the reader wants to keep reading!

⚡

Middle — The Problem

This is where something exciting happens! The character faces a problem or challenge. This is the most important part — it keeps the reader interested!

🌟

End — The Solution

The character solves the problem. The story comes to a satisfying close. The reader should feel happy or satisfied at the end!

🎭 The 4 Elements of a Great Story

👤 Characters

The people or animals in your story. Give them names and describe what they look like!

🏠 Setting

WHERE and WHEN the story happens. A forest, a city, a school — be specific!

⚠️ Problem

Something goes wrong or a challenge appears. Without a problem, there is no story!

✅ Solution

How the character fixes the problem. Make it clever or surprising!

📖 Read this Story carefully!

Notice how it has a clear beginning, middle and end!

🦁 The Lost Lion Cub

🔵 Beginning
Once upon a time, in the heart of the African savannah, there lived a young lion cub named Simba. He had golden fur and bright curious eyes. One sunny morning, Simba wandered too far from his family while chasing a butterfly.
🟢 Middle
Suddenly, Simba realised he was lost. The tall grass looked the same in every direction. He called out for his mother but heard no reply. A kind old elephant named Tembo heard his cries. "Do not worry, little one," said Tembo gently. "I know this land well. I will help you find your family."
🟡 End
Tembo lifted his trunk and let out a great trumpet sound. Soon, Simba's mother came running through the grass. She nuzzled her cub lovingly. Simba thanked Tembo with a happy roar. From that day on, Simba never wandered too far from home — but he always remembered his kind friend Tembo.
👤 Characters
Simba (lion cub), Tembo (elephant), Simba's mother
🏠 Setting
African savannah, sunny morning
⚠️ Problem
Simba gets lost chasing a butterfly
✅ Solution
Tembo helps find Simba's mother
